Announcements

Welcome to NMCC Summer Coding Class!
May - August, 2022

Welcome to the coding class, a basic introductory course on python. Since we have a diverse audience/student ranging from 9 to 18 years, we will be moving at a medium speed so that we carry everyone along and I hope you'll learn a lot from this class. As you know, this is not a graded class (so there is no pressure on you), just try to have as much fun as you can. We will mostly be meeting over zoom (ZOOM LINK) and meet once in a while on Sundays at the Masjid (will let you know when). We would also be having a daily quiz posted on our WhatsApp group (JOIN WhatsApp). You can as well ask ANY QUESTIONS regarding what has been discussed or the class in general. Remember, it has to be INTERACTIVE and your input is required.

We'll update this page with more information as we get closer to the start of the class. In the meantime, feel free to email me at biala4mat@gmail.com if you have any questions about the class!
